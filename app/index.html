Topics

Topics are a way of sending notification to multiple users. For example every shop has its own topic and sub topics. For example shop1, shop1_blog, shop1_webinars, etc.

You can add people to a topic by simply sending a POST request to https://iid.googleapis.com/iid/v1/TOKEN/rel/topics/TOPIC
